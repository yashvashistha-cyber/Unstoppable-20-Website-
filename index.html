About Us

Every great change starts with a single thought. Inspired by a young girl studying on the streets of Udaipur in 2017, founders Ram Raibari and Rahul Singh envisioned a future where every child, regardless of their background, could access quality education.

On 27 October 2020, Unstoppable-20 Foundation began its journey in Pratap Nagar, Jaipur, with just 7 children. Despite early challenges, the dedication of our volunteers and the trust of local families helped us grow into a thriving community.

Today, we proudly support 270+ children across two learning centers, with the help of 30 passionate teachers and 70 volunteers. Beyond free education, we empower children through computer education, spoken English, digital literacy, life skills, personality development, Vedic mathematics, and future-ready technologies like AI, robotics, and drones.

Our mission is simple: to educate, empower, and inspire every child to build a brighter future. From 7 children to 270+ dreams — our journey has just begun. 💙

  • Quality Education: Curriculum tailored to foundational literacy and numeracy.
  • Digital Access: Laptop-based computer training in 102/48, Sector 10, Pratap Nagar, Jaipur.
  • Outside Activities: Movies, visits to famous places in Jaipur, and fun outdoor experiences for every child.
